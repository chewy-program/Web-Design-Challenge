Comparing Temperature to Wind Speed

The closest of any two relationships is the relationship between temperature and windspeed. This relationship is inverse, in that they are both related to Longtitude. In the case of temperature, it generally increases the closer Longtitude is to 0, where as windspeed generally decreases the closer it is to 0.
